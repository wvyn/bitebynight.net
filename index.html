Bite By Night

Survive the night... or become the hunter.

About the Game

Bite By Night is a Roblox survival horror game where players take on the role of Survivors or a Killer. Survivors must complete objectives and escape before 6 AM, while the Killer hunts them down.

Gameplay

  • Choose between Survivor or Killer
  • Work as a team or hunt alone
  • Use abilities and strategy to win

Features

  • Multiple classes (Healer, Fighter, Security Guard, etc.)
  • Unique killers with special abilities
  • Dark, immersive horror maps
